About the Author

Andrea Valentini was born in Grottaglie, a small town in the province of Taranto, Italy. As a licensed physiotherapist, Andrea continues his professional growth in order to create a sustained connection with his clients, their physical challenges and their ultimate recovery. He learned how to accomplish this goal by acting as a link between the science of his work and the specific needs of his clientele.

Andrea is passionate about neurology, neuropsychology, orthopedics, and a wide variety of successful and sustainable rehabilitative techniques. All of this is combined with a high degree of empathy for his clients from both a human and psychophysical point of view.

The art and science of PHYSIOTHERAPY

Physiotherapy is a science that specifically helps anyone who suffers, or has previously suffered, a physical or neurological injury requiring rehabilitation, and whose aim is to improve their overall physical condition. This is accomplished by ensuring the client's active participation in their own recovery including a full understanding of their role in the process. After recovery has been achieved, the Physiotherapist will often advise their client on methods to help ensure a pain free, post-recovery life-style.
